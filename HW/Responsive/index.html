Thugger Takes to Twitter

Thugger Thug.

"Thugger Thug" was actually coined by Snoop Dogg in a collaboration song with Young Thug called "Get High". The song begins with a rather interesting intro and outro, that include both Snoop and Thug sparking a few joints and venting.

Jeffery.

Jeffery is Young Thug's main alias in the industry. He uses it to describe his more creative persona that tends to find itself dipping its toe into R&B, Rock, Gospel, and Contemporary.

Slime.

Slime takes on many forms in the world of southern rap. For Young Thug and members of YSL Records, it is a sign of a brotherhood and community with similar style, sound, missions and goals.
